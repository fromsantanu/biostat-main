### **ğŸ“ Key Formula**

For **n pairs of data (Xi, Yi)**:

$$
\rho = 1 - \frac{6 \sum d_i^2}{n(n^2 - 1)}

Where:

* **d\_i:** Difference between ranks of Xi and Yi
* **n:** Number of observations
